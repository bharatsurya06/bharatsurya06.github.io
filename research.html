My Research

My research focuses on cryogenic energy recovery systems, thermal performance optimization, and advanced energy storage solutions. I am passionate about developing innovative approaches to improve energy efficiency and sustainability in engineering systems. My work involves both experimental and computational studies, aiming to bridge the gap between theory and practical application.
